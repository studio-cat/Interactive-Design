Day 2.

He is so gracious.
He shows me grace
from my target of bitterness.
He uses the most unworthy,
the most weak

He teaches an upside down world
Nothing makes sense,
but at the same time,
everything makes complete,
perfect, sense.

the way,
the truth
the life

A Seed must Die.

What does it mean to
die to myself daily?
What an irony it is that
it's easier to die for someone
than to die to yourself
for their sake
