PREFACE


upside down world





who am i, and who are you? rather, what are we? merely coincidental collections of atoms that happen to be able to think, feel and act? yet why do you all work so hard? what is it that you're striving towards?


what is it that you see that i cannot?


it is too noisy outside.
what is the truth? which way is right? is there any meaning to this all?



.



.



.



LAMENT is for those who, too, wrestle with these questions.

it is a testimony of how i have found,
and am drowning in the infinite depths of the grace of,

the truth.
